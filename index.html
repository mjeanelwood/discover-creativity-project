What is creativity?

There are numerous definitions and opinions as to what creativity is. For some it follows artistic traits such as writing & painting. Others can be technically creative finding new ways to improve the "mouse trap" or inventing a new one. Creativity can be seen as an ability; you can either have a natural ability to perform, or you can learn and improve on what you have.

We all have the seed of creativity within us. Some water and feed that seed until it grows into something beautiful and wonderous. Some will have a seed that only grows to a smaller vision but still satisfies that creative desire within themselves. Whatever seed of creativity you have within you the important thing to remember is to have fun with it!
